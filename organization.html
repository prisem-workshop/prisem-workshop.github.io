Organizing Committee

Dmitry Kudryavtsev, Graduate School of Management St. Petersburg University and Smart Architects, Russia
Dmitry Kudryavtsev is an Associate Professor, Information Technologies in Management Department, Graduate School of Management, Saint-Petersburg University. Candidate of Science in Computer Science, 2009. MBAE (Master of Business Administration and Engineering), 2002. Dmitry has 80+ publications (20 are indexed in Scopus; 4 text-books). He was a lead researcher and co-investivator in 10+ projects, funded by RFBR, RSF, Ministry for Economic Development and Trade of the RF, Department of Information Technology of Moscow city government etc. Dmitry worked as a senior consultant & scientific director at consulting company “Business Engineering Group” (2003-2015), now he is also working as a senior consultant & head of R&D at consulting company “Smart Architects” (2018-present). Cofounder, Head of Research and Education, Inter-university Academic Center of Competence on Enterprise Architecture «EA Lab» in Russia (2015 – present). His research interests focus on enterprise architecture management, business modeling, knowledge management, knowledge engineering, ontological engineering.
Gil Regev, EPFL and Itecor, Switzerland
Gil Regev joined EPFL in 1997 as Senior Researcher after 9 years at Logitech Switzerland and USA. Gil obtained his Ph.D. in Communication Systems in 2003. Since 2008 Gil works both at EPFL and Itecor. Vice President of the Swiss Knowledge Management Forum. He does research, teaching and consulting in Requirements Engineering, Business modeling, Enterprise Architecture, Knowledge Management, creativity in organizations, and the way they influence information systems design. These topics all relate to the organization's understanding of itself and of its environment, hence my interest in Systems Thinking.
Blagovesta Kostova, EPFL, Switzerland
Blagovesta Kostova is a PhD student at EPFL in the group of Prof. Alain Wegmann. She is the president of LauzHack an event that brings together 300 developers from all over the world and many industrial sponsors to EPFL for a programming competition. She has substantial industrial experience and has published papers at several conferences and workshops: RE, RCIS, ISPIM, etc. Her main research interest is service-oriented modeling of complex systems, and in particular in the field of engineering privacy by design.
Mike Rosen, Business Architecture Guild, USA
Mike Rosen is Chief Scientist at Wilton Consulting Group which provides expert consulting in Digital Transformation Strategy, Business Architecture, Enterprise Architecture, and Enterprise Solutions. Mr. Rosen's 30 year career has spanned startups, CTO roles, chief architect, and product architect for industry leading middleware products, software development, and hardware design. Mr. Rosen is also a founding member, Board Director, and Vice President of the Business Architecture Guild. Prior to returning to consulting, Mr. Rosen was Global Research VP for IDC, providing research to IT and business leaders on strategic architecture and digital transformation. 2018.
Geert Poels, Gent University, Belgium
Geert Poels is head of UGentMIS, which he founded in 2005. He is full professor of Management Information Systems (since October 1, 2012) and member of the professorial staff of the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration, Ghent University (since October 1, 2002). He is member of the University Research Council. He also teaches and directs master dissertation research at IC Institute (Beersel, Belgium). Within UGentMIS he coordinates the Enterprise Modelling research cluster. In the period 2005-2019 he was promoter of 13 completed PhD research projects (11 at UGent and 2 at KU Leuven), while currently he is promoter of 10 ongoing PhD projects. End 2019 he has 119 publications listed in Web of Science with an h-index of 14. His Scopus h-index is 19. His Google Scholar h-index is 31 with over 3300 citations recorded.
